Design Recommendations

Typography: 2 Font System

Space Grotesk (headings) + Inter (body/UI). Eliminare Lato e Montserrat. Piu tech-native, piu coerente.

AI Gradient

Purple → Cyan linear-gradient(135deg, #7A00DF, #06B6D4) ovunque l'AI e' protagonista. Signature visiva.

Dark Mode

Background #0F0F1A con tinta purple. Per hero sections, pricing, feature showcase.

Icon System

Icone proprietarie: architettura + AI. Outlined 2px, 24px grid, 4px radius. Micro-animazioni hover.

Motion Design

Scroll animations, number counters animati, AI "breathing" pulse, before/after gesture-enabled.

Tech Signals

Grid overlay, glassmorphism cards, monospace per KPI, purple glow su elementi AI attivi.
